#  Inventory Management MIS Project

##  Project Overview

This project simulates the role of an MIS Executive responsible for managing inventory, analyzing sales trends, and improving operational efficiency.

The goal is to identify:

* Stock shortages
* Demand vs supply gaps
* Supplier risks
* Warehouse efficiency
* Revenue trends

---

##  Tools Used

* **Excel** → Data cleaning & preprocessing
* **SQL (PostgreSQL)** → Data analysis
* **Power BI** → Dashboard & visualization

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains:

* Product details
* Stock transactions (in/out)
* Sales data
* Supplier information

---

##  Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Current Stock Calculation

* Calculated stock using:
  `Stock In - Stock Out`

### 2. Demand vs Supply Mismatch

* Compared total sales with current stock
* Identified high-demand, low-stock products

### 3. Inventory Turnover

* Measured how fast inventory is sold

### 4. Warehouse Efficiency

* Analyzed stock movement across warehouses

### 5. Supplier Risk Analysis

* Evaluated suppliers based on:

  * Lead time
  * Reliability score

### 6. Monthly Sales Trend

* Used window functions to track growth

---

##  Dashboard Features (Power BI)

* KPI Cards → Total Sales, Stock, Turnover
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Product-wise Demand vs Stock
* Supplier Risk Overview
  

---

##  Key Insights

* Identified products with **high demand but low stock**
* Found **slow-moving inventory**
* Highlighted **high-risk suppliers**
* Observed **seasonal sales trends**

---

##  Business Impact

This analysis helps:

* Avoid stockouts
* Improve supplier selection
* Optimize inventory levels
* Increase operational efficiency
